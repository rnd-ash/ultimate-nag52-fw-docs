ShiftPhase ShiftData::overlap_data

Shift overlap phase New clutches are moved into place and old once released SPC now controls new clutches, MPC is relaxed.

Duration and pressure during the overlap phase affects shift feeling

Duration: Longer = slower gear change SPC Ramp: Bigger = firmer shift
